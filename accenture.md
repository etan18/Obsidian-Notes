- Profiles
	- strategic integrator: early adopters, high confidence, high usage of AI, perceived ability
	- cautious optimizer: moderate usage, lower confidence, transparency and privacy concerns are important
	- operational striver: fragmented use, putting in effort but not getting the same output from AI
	- disconnected skeptic: low trust, minimal engagement
- Profiles were assembled from maturity evals across 5 axes
	- AI use in workflow
	- Accessibility, culture, is this person enabled/blocked from adoption
	- Efficiency
	- Knowledge/maturity
- Goal: propose new interaction paradigm, behavioral nudges for users to build trust and comfort over time
	- Two environments: text only input, mixed initiative interaction
