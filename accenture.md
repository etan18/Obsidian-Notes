CITI socio-behavioral training ![[accenture.png]]


Focus:
- Enterprise workflows (bespoke tools, SAP Joule, Agentforce)
	- Toggling tax: tools are highly specialized and diffused
	- Decision support tools, autonomous agents w/ human in the loop, chat interfaces
- Profiles
	- strategic integrator: early adopters, high confidence, high usage of AI, perceived ability
	- cautious optimizer: moderate usage, lower confidence, transparency and privacy concerns are important
	- operational striver: fragmented use, putting in effort but not getting the same output from AI
	- disconnected skeptic: low trust, minimal engagement
- Goal: propose new interaction paradigm, behavioral nudges for users
