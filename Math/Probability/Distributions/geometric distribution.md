The **geometric distribution** is a *memoryless* distribution that finds the time to first success. Given a [[random variable]] $X \sim Geo(p)$, we have
$$ \mathbb{P}[X=k] = (1-p)^{k - 1}\cdot p $$
with expectation and variance
$$ \mathbb{E}[X] = \frac1p $$
$$Var(X) = \frac{1-p}{p^2}$$
